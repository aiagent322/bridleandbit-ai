The Bridle & Bit Network

Bridle & Bit Magazine is Arizona's premier horse publication — a tabloid-format magazine averaging 120+ pages per monthly issue, distributed free in print and digital. Since 1978, it has served the western performance horse community with in-depth coverage of reining, cutting, cowhorse, team roping, barrel racing, ranch riding, rodeo, and the equestrian lifestyle.

The AI Division extends that reach across 9 Facebook Groups with over 61,600 combined members and 6 AI-powered websites targeting the equestrian market. Together, this network gives advertisers access to the most dedicated, active, and high-intent horse audience in the Southwest.

The Audience

Bridle & Bit readers and community members are active participants in the horse industry — not casual browsers. They own horses, haul trailers, buy tack, hire trainers, book stalls, insure livestock, and purchase property. They are buyers, not window shoppers.

  • Horse owners and competitors — team ropers, barrel racers, reiners, cutters, ranch riders, and all-around competitors
  • Trainers and professionals — NRHA, NCHA, AQHA, and USTRC professionals who influence purchasing decisions
  • Breeders and ranch owners — standing stallions, raising foals, and managing equestrian operations
  • Property buyers and sellers — actively searching for or listing horse properties in Arizona
  • Event attendees — competing and spectating at WestWorld, HorseShoe Park, and venues statewide

AI-Powered Websites

The AI Division maintains 6 web properties purpose-built for the equestrian community. Each site targets specific high-intent audiences:

TeamRoping.ai

The largest free team roping events calendar in the US — updated near-daily from real producer flyers with "Near Me" geolocation search. Reaches ropers nationwide across Arizona, Texas, Oklahoma, New Mexico, California, and beyond.

HorseCalendar.ai

AI-powered Arizona equine event calendar covering shows, clinics, rodeos, and competitions statewide. The go-to resource for Arizona horse event planning.

HorseInsurance.ai

Comprehensive horse insurance guide with hundreds of Q&A entries, a 108-term glossary, and blog posts. Attracts horse owners researching mortality, liability, and farm coverage.

PodcastCorral.com

Equestrian podcast directory with 129 shows across 13 categories. Reaches the growing audio audience in the horse world.

TeamRopingVideos.com

Curated team roping video library — competition footage, training tips, and instructional content for the roping community.

WorldsGreatestHorseman.com

Historical archive and champion profiles from the legendary World's Greatest Horseman competition.
