The "Near Me" Search That Changes Everything

Stop scrolling through endless Facebook flyers hoping to find a roping this weekend. TeamRoping.ai's "Near Me" geolocation search uses your location to surface every upcoming roping within your chosen radius — 100 miles, 250 miles, or 500 miles. Tap once and see what's close.

Filter by state, search by arena name, or browse the full nationwide calendar. Every listing includes the details ropers actually need: date, location, entry fees, class format, contact info, and arena coordinates. Events span team roping, breakaway, ranch roping, jackpots, futurities, rodeos, and clinics.
