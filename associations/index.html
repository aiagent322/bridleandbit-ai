Team Roping

5 Orgs
USTRC
United States Team Roping Championships
Team Roping
The world's largest team roping organization, sanctioning hundreds of ropings annually across numbered handicap divisions. Founded in 1989 — the go-to for amateur ropers coast to coast.
ustrc.com Guthrie, OK →
WSTR
World Series of Team Roping
Team Roping
One of the premier team roping circuits with massive jackpot events and a championship finale. Known for drawing top amateur competitors and life-changing payouts.
wstr.com Scottsdale, AZ →
NTR
National Team Roping
Team Roping
A growing team roping association running events at arenas across the Southwest and beyond. Popular with Arizona ropers for local circuit events and year-end finals.
ntr.com Southwest US →
BFI
Best of the Best / Bob Feist Invitational
Team Roping
The richest team roping in the world. Held annually in Reno, NV — the BFI draws the top amateur ropers competing for million-dollar-plus prize pools across all handicap numbers.
bfi.com Reno, NV →
TRAA
Team Roping Arizona Association
Team Roping
Arizona's home-state team roping association running local jackpots, ropings, and series events at venues across the Valley and statewide. Strong presence at arenas like HorsePower and Southwest.
Arizona-based Arizona →

Rodeo

2 Orgs
PRCA
Professional Rodeo Cowboys Association
Rodeo
The world's oldest and largest rodeo sanctioning organization. Governs professional rodeo competition in all standard events — bull riding, tie-down roping, steer wrestling, team roping, and more. Home of the NFR.
prorodeo.com Colorado Springs, CO →
WPRA
Women's Professional Rodeo Association
Rodeo
The sanctioning body for women's barrel racing at PRCA-approved rodeos. Governs the barrel racing event at the National Finals Rodeo and runs the world standings for professional barrel racers.
wpra.com Colorado Springs, CO →

NRHA
National Reining Horse Association
Reining
The international governing body for the sport of reining. Sanctions competitions worldwide, maintains world standings, and hosts the NRHA Futurity — one of the richest equine events in the world at Oklahoma City.
nrha.com Oklahoma City, OK →

Barrel Racing

Breed & Registry

2 Orgs
AQHA
American Quarter Horse Association
Breed / Registry
The world's largest equine breed registry with over 3 million members and 6 million registered horses. Sanctions horse shows, racing, and performance events across all western and English disciplines.
aqha.com Amarillo, TX →
APHA
American Paint Horse Association
Breed / Registry
The second-largest equine breed registry in the US. Sanctions western and English performance competition, halter classes, and youth events for Paint horses of all patterns.
apha.com Fort Worth, TX →

English

1 Org
NCHA
National Cutting Horse Association
Cutting
The world governing body for cutting horse competition. Sanctions thousands of events annually and crowns world champions at the iconic Futurity held each December in Fort Worth, Texas.
ncha.com Fort Worth, TX →
NRCHA
National Reined Cow Horse Association
Reined Cow Horse
Governs reined cow horse competition — a discipline combining reining, cow work, and fence work. Home of the Snaffle Bit Futurity, one of the richest events in western performance.
nrcha.com Temecula, CA →
PBR
Professional Bull Riders
Bull Riding
The world's premier bull riding organization, featuring the toughest bulls and most talented riders on the planet. The PBR World Finals determines the world champion each November in Las Vegas.
pbr.com Pueblo, CO →
BLM
Bureau of Land Management
Wild Horse Program
Federal agency managing America's wild horse and burro populations across the western United States. Oversees adoption programs, herd management areas, and the National Wild Horse and Burro Program.
blm.gov Washington, DC →
USDA
U.S. Department of Agriculture
Federal / Equine Health
Federal oversight of equine health, disease monitoring, and interstate movement regulations. The USDA APHIS division is the primary authority on equine infectious disease control and import/export requirements.
usda.gov Washington, DC →
USEF
United States Equestrian Federation
English
The national governing body for equestrian sport in the United States. Sanctions competitions across hunter/jumper, dressage, eventing, endurance, and more. Also oversees the US Olympic equestrian program.
usef.org Lexington, KY →
